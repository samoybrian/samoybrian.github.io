College Learnings

Lessons Learned from the College Life, Career Goals, Personal




Time management and how to ask for help

I learned the value of setting priorities and practicing discipline through being on a team. Getting advice is a crucial part of growing.



The importance of teamwork and friendship

Being a member of a team entails collaborating with individuals of varied backgrounds to achieve a common objective. In today's world, those same leadership and collaborative abilities are priceless.



Don't put limits on yourself

I worked my lone all-nighter to complete my thesis paper at the end of my senior year. There are always reasons not to do something, therefore I practically daily reflect on this event. The biggest obstacle to achieving our potential is frequently our mindset.



You are your greatest advocate

Because I had discovered an occasion and a setting that were better fits for me, my times and stress levels both decreased. Find a way to make things work if it's something you're enthusiastic about.



Everything really does happen for a reason

This may be the most overused lesson in the book but thinking back on my time in college helps me understand cause and effect in a very concrete way and helps me see how living a normal life, with all its joy and uncertainty, can ultimately teach us valuable things.
